This will probably end up one very long page. But setting that aside, welcome! I've always had an intrest in old Japanese ever since I started studying the language, particularly towards that of the hentaigana and sōsho writing. To me the hentaigana looked as if they were just scribles and lines across the page, like writing sans meaning.
